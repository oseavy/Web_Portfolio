ABOUT OLIVIA

Hello! My name is Olivia Seavy.

I grew up in the Philadelphia area and graduated from Christopher Dock Mennonite High School in 2015. Currently, I am studying architecture as a first year within the College of Fine Arts at Carnegie Mellon University.

I really enjoy art, history, and writing. I also am very passionate about sports and fitness, as I am also a proud member of the women's soccer team here at CMU. Go Tartans!
